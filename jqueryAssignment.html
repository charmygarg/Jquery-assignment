Akka

Akka is a free and open-source toolkit and runtime simplifying the construction of concurrent and distributed applications on the JVM. Language bindings exist for both Java and Scala. Akka is written in Scala

Scala

Scala is an acronym for “Scalable Language”. This means that Scala grows with you. You can play with it by typing one-line expressions and observing the results. There’s a REPL and IDE worksheets for quick feedback.

Play

Play is based on a lightweight, stateless, web-friendly architecture.Built on Akka, Play provides predictable and minimal resource consumption (CPU, memory, threads) for highly-scalable applications.

Spark

Apache® Spark™ is a powerful open source processing engine built around speed, ease of use, and sophisticated analytics. It was originally developed at UC Berkeley in 2009.Apache Spark is 100% open source.
